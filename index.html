About Us

Welcome to the Robotics Club of SRMCEM, where innovation meets engineering excellence! We are a dynamic community of aspiring engineers, tech enthusiasts, and creative minds working together to explore the fascinating world of robotics.

Our club provides a platform for students to gain hands-on experience in robotics, participate in competitions, and work on cutting-edge projects. From building autonomous robots to developing innovative solutions, we're committed to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in robotics.
